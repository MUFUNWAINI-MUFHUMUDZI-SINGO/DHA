Birth Certificates

Welcome to the Birth Certificates section of the Department of Home Affairs. Here you can find comprehensive information on how to apply for, renew, and manage birth certificates.

How to Apply

To apply for a birth certificate, follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the application form available at our offices or online.
  2. Submit the completed form along with the required documents.
  3. Pay the applicable fee.
  4. Wait for the processing period, after which you will receive your birth certificate.

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • Original or certified copy of the birth notification.
  • Proof of identity of the applicant.
  • Proof of residence.

Fees and Processing Times

The fees for birth certificates vary based on the type of service required. Processing times can also vary. Please refer to our tariffs page for detailed information.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact us through our Contact Page.
