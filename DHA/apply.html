For Replacement of Existing ID Card

General Steps for Both Scenarios

Login to your account on the online portal.

Navigate to the Application Section for ID card application or replacement.

Fill in all required and additional information as needed.

Upload necessary documents or photographs.

Review and submit your application.

Schedule a biometric appointment if required.
