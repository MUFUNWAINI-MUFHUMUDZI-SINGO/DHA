Marriage Certificates

Welcome to the Marriage Certificates section of the Department of Home Affairs. Here you can find comprehensive information about obtaining, renewing, and managing marriage certificates.

Eligibility Requirements

To apply for a marriage certificate, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be legally married according to South African law.
  • Both parties must be present or represented by a legal representative.
  • Provide valid identification and proof of marriage.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for a marriage certificate:

  1. Complete the marriage certificate application form, available online or at DHA offices.
  2. Submit the form with the necessary documents at your nearest DHA office or online.
  3. Pay the applicable fee. Details on fees can be found on our tariffs page.
  4. Await processing. Processing times may vary. Check the status of your application online or contact our office.

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents:

  • Original or certified copy of the marriage certificate.
  • Identification documents (e.g., ID book, passport).
  • Proof of residence.
  • Proof of marital status if applicable (e.g., divorce decree, death certificate of a spouse).

Service Locations

You can apply for marriage certificates at any Department of Home Affairs office across South Africa. For a full list of locations, please visit our Service Locations page.
